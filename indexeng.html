About me



My name is Javier Cumbreras García. I did an Audiovisual Communications degree in Málaga (Spain) and I'm currently finishing a post-degree about videogames designing.

Since 2020 I have developed some videogames, most of them making small teams and some on my own. I have also participated in a few Jams. I've developed most of my games in Unity, so I'm really used to the engine, in which I've programmed a lot in C#; and for my actual project I'm using Unreal 5 and I've done some experiments in it, so I consider myself to have a pretty decent level on both engines.

I am really enthusiastic every time I have the oportunity to be creative, so I'm just in love with videogame design. I have good communication skills and I'm used to tools like Trello, Notion, Miro or Tom's Planner. Most of the games I've' made have been developed in teams, so I am used to work with other people and I'm good at it, but I've face individual tasks and projects too.

Pestilence

Pestilence (currently in development) is a first person stealth game in which you encarnate a rotten god who reach his objectives possessing people and using his dark powers.

This is my first time being part of a big team for a project. As a designer I've taken part in feature, quest, movility, AI behaviour and level design. I've also done some blueprints to prototype some of the features (which then are replaced with the programmers code) and implement some of the systems. I'm the sound designer too and I'm composing part of the soundtrack. To this date I'm finishing the sound features and doing Q&A.

Heart of the Forest

Heart of the Forest An isometric tower defense, where you embody the spirit guardian of the forest, who must protect the pond from the incoming attacks of the fungal kingdom.

This game was created during the TecnoCampus Mataró GGJ23 where we received the award of best music and sound.

I participated in designing and programming tasks, and I also did the sound design and music composing.

Download at itch.io

Raíces

Raíces is a decision-making and resource management game, in which you'll have to take care of a small sprout until it becomes a large hundred-year-old tree. We invite you to play it several times to discover all the possible events.

This project has been the result of our participation in the Indie Spain Jam 2022, where it has been awarded in the "best use of the theme" category.

On this project, I was the quest designer and the soundtrack composer, but I also programmed a few features.

Play it at itch.io

Until One Falls

Until One Falls (Currently in Development) is an online platform fight game in which you can place your own platforms wherever you want, using them to help you and put your rival at a disadvantage.

On this project I've done designing and programming tasks at 50% in team with a partner. We've used Photon for the online implementation and we just have to fix some bugs and polish a few features for it to be finished.

Silent Hospital II

Silent Hospital II First person shooter. You will have to scape from this hospital, full of soldiers with different behaviours. This is the first project where I tried to make enemies with more complex behaviours, implementing AIs. Entirely developed by me.

Download at itch.io GitHub Repo

Pequeñas Sospechas

Pequeñas Sospechas is a mini game developed by Pego Games for the Málaga Game Jam Weekend 14. Agent Rodríguez receives a call, her co-worker needs to be covered by doing lineups at the police station. You must read the reports and consult the testimonies of the suspects to determine who was the culprit in each case. But beware, nothing is what it seems.

I was the lead designer of this project. I designed the 'quests' and write all the descriptions. I composed the soundtrack too.

Play it at itch.io

The Last Tree

The Last Tree. Some mysterious woodcutting robots have destroyed your tree friends. Take revenge and find out what is happening. In this 2D platform you will have to combat many different enemies.

This is my first full developed game. I did it in team with a friend. Although the main and general decisions were tooken with my partner I was the lead designer so I did level design, enemies and movility features. I also programmed a lot of features of the game, including some enemies and the boss, and I composed the game's soundtrack too (my first time for a videogame).

Download at itch.io
